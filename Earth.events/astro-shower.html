Comet Flyby

Alignment: A comet flyby occurs when a comet’s orbit brings it close to Earth, passing through the inner solar system near our planet.
Appearance: From Earth, the comet appears as a bright nucleus with a glowing tail streaming away from the Sun, moving across the sky.
Rarity: Close comet flybys are uncommon; while comets regularly orbit the Sun, only a few pass near Earth within a human lifetime.
