Mercury Transit

Alignment: A transit happens when Mercury, Venus (the only other planet that can do this from Earth), the Sun, and Earth align
Appearance: Mercury looks like a small black speck crossing the bright solar disk.
Rarity: Due to orbital tilts, these alignments are infrequent, averaging about 13 per 100 years, and only in May or November.
